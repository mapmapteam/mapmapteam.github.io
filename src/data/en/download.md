## Launching the Application

**macOS:** Open the application by double-clicking on its icon in the Finder, or by clicking on its icon in the Dock.

**Linux (Debian/Ubuntu):** Find its icon in the application menu and click it. You can also run it via the command-line by typing `mapmap`.

**Windows:** Click on the Windows icon and type in mapmap. Click on the MapMap icon to launch the program.
